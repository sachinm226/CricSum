Dwayne Bravo led West Indies to an important victory in an abbreviated contest ? DigicelCricket.com In 20.1 overs West Indies transformed from a team that lost the Test series 2-0 to an outfit that will ride a surge of confidence into the five-match one-day series beginning in St Vincent on Tuesday. The seven-wicket Twenty20 victory in a rain-reduced game had the Barbados supporters dancing in the stands and Dwayne Bravo, the temporary captain, was also excited. "It's nice to come out on top, I must give credit to everyone, the guys played tremendous from the start," Bravo said after play.
